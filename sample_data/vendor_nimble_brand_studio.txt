SCOPE OF WORK
================================================================================

LINE ITEM 1: Strategy & Creative Development — INCLUDED
---------------------------------------------------------
Complete brand strategy, consumer insights synthesis, and 3 campaign concepts.
We work fast — strategy delivered in 2 weeks.

Additional service at no extra charge: We will also develop a social media brand
playbook (tone of voice, visual identity for social, platform-specific guidelines).
This is typically billed separately by other agencies. We include it because we
believe it makes the campaign stronger.

Note: We may also propose an influencer campaign strategy as an extension of the
brand strategy — this could be an exciting add-on for NourishPlus if the budget
allows post initial campaign review.

LINE ITEM 2: TVC Development — INCLUDED
-----------------------------------------
- 2 x 30-second TVC scripts (Hindi)
- 2 x 10-second cutdown scripts
- Full storyboards

We develop fast, lean scripts. First draft delivered in 1 week. Maximum 1 round
of client revisions included. Additional revisions: INR 50,000 per round.

LINE ITEM 3: TVC Production — INCLUDED
----------------------------------------
We produce TVCs using our partner production network in Pune and Mumbai.

- 2 x 30-second TVCs + 2 x 10-second cutdowns
- 1 shoot day (all scenes and formats to be completed in 1 day)
- Post-production: editing, colour, sound
- Digital delivery formats

Our approach: We plan shoots meticulously so that 1 shoot day is sufficient. If
additional shoot days are needed (scope change), each additional day is billed at
INR 4,00,000.

Note on casting: Child actor casting handled through our Pune partner network.
ASCI-required parental consent documentation is the responsibility of the production
house partner (Lens & Light Productions, Pune).

Broadcast delivery: We deliver in HD MP4 for digital platforms. DITTO/broadcast-
specific delivery formats (for DD Freedish, Star, etc.) can be arranged at additional
cost — please confirm if broadcast TV delivery specs are required.

LINE ITEM 4: Social Organic Content — INCLUDED
------------------------------------------------
- 60 posts/month across Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube for 6 months (360 total)
- Content calendar and client approval via WhatsApp/email
- 10 nano-influencer collaborations per month — briefing and coordination only

We match the 60 posts/month requirement. Content will be planned in batches of 30 to
ensure efficiency.

Influencer fees: nano-influencer compensation is NOT included. We estimate INR 3,000-
8,000 per post. At 10 influencers/month this could be INR 30,000-80,000/month —
client to budget separately.

Note: For YouTube organic, we recommend repurposing content from other platforms.
Dedicated YouTube-original long-form content is not included at this price point.

Additional opportunity: If NourishPlus is open to micro-influencer campaigns
(INR 50K-2L per influencer), we can design and execute a separate influencer
amplification program. This would be an exciting extension of the base campaign.

LINE ITEM 5: Social Paid Media Planning — INCLUDED
----------------------------------------------------
- Channel strategy: Meta and Google/YouTube (primary)
- Audience targeting framework
- Budget model
- KPI plan

Note: Our paid media planning is solid but lean — a 20-30 page strategy document
rather than a 60-page exhaustive model. We find clients prefer actionable and concise
over comprehensive and unused.

LINE ITEM 6: Social Paid Media Buying & Optimization — INCLUDED
-----------------------------------------------------------------
- Campaign management on Meta and Google
- Weekly performance report via WhatsApp dashboard summary
- Monthly review call

Fee: Included in overall project price. No additional commission.

Note: At media spend above INR 50L/month, a supplementary management fee of INR
2,00,000/month will apply (to be discussed and agreed separately at campaign start).

LINE ITEM 7: Kids Advertising & Claims Compliance — INCLUDED
-------------------------------------------------------------
Our creatives follow ASCI guidelines. We are fully aware of kids advertising rules.

Every creative asset goes through our internal brand safety checklist before delivery.
We ensure all content is age-appropriate, parent-friendly, and brand-safe.

FSSAI health claims: we review all claims against FSSAI guidelines as part of our
standard creative QC process.

Our team has built campaigns for kids and family products including Mango Bite,
Kurkure Junior, and a Tier-2 education startup. We understand the space.

LINE ITEM 8: Launch Program Management — INCLUDED
---------------------------------------------------

- Shared tracker on Google Sheets

We keep it simple and responsive. Our founder (Karan Shah) is personally available
