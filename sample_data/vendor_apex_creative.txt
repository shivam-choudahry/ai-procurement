EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

Apex Creative Co. is pleased to submit this comprehensive proposal for the NourishKids
brand launch. With 18 years in FMCG marketing — including breakthrough campaigns for
Nestle India, Britannia, Marico, and Dabur — we bring deep category knowledge and
proven regulatory navigation capability to this mandate.

Our proposed investment: INR 4,20,00,000 (excluding GST and media buying commission),
covering all 8 line items in the RFQ.

We believe NourishKids has a differentiated story to tell: nutrition without compromise,
in a format kids actually want. Our campaign platform — "Snack Smart, Grow Strong" —
was developed as a concept during our briefing process and is available for discussion
at the presentation stage.

SECTION 1: SCOPE OF WORK RESPONSE
================================================================================

LINE ITEM 1: Strategy and Creative Development
-----------------------------------------------
Deliverables:
- Full brand strategy document (positioning, proposition, reason-to-believe for
  parent + child audiences)
- Campaign creative brief and creative platform
- 3 creative concept directions with full rationale and visual references
- Key messaging architecture (parent audience, child audience, retailer)
- Visual identity guidelines for the NourishKids campaign

Assumptions: We will work from NourishPlus brand guidelines and market research to be
shared at kickoff. Primary research (if required beyond what is shared) is not included.

Timeline: Weeks 1-6 from project kickoff

LINE ITEM 2: TVC Development
------------------------------
Deliverables:
- 2 x 30-second TVC scripts — one in Hindi, one adapted to English
- 4 x 10-second cutdown scripts (2 Hindi, 2 English)
- Full storyboards for all 6 formats
- Animatic for client approval before production

Important note: The English TVC will be a language adaptation of the Hindi master TVC.
Both will be broadcast-ready but share the same core narrative and visual concept. We
do not develop independent creative routes per language as standard practice.

Timeline: Weeks 4-10 from kickoff

LINE ITEM 3: TVC Production
-----------------------------
Deliverables:
- Full production of 2 x 30-second TVCs + 4 x 10-second cutdowns
- Casting: minimum 20 child actors shortlisted; 5 selected with parental consent
  documentation
- Location scouting: minimum 2 options in Mumbai or Delhi presented
- Director: from our empanelled roster (primary and backup director options confirmed)
- Post-production: colour grading, sound design, broadcast format delivery (HD + SD +
  digital)
- ASCI compliance certificate for broadcast

Production note: Each TVC includes 1 day of principal photography. Additional shoot
days, if required due to scope change, are billed at INR 8 lakhs per day. Out-of-city
locations (outside Mumbai/Delhi) attract travel and accommodation at cost.

Timeline: Weeks 8-18 from kickoff

LINE ITEM 4: Social Organic Content
-------------------------------------
Deliverables:
- 60 posts/month across Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube for 6 months
- Monthly content calendar submitted 15 days in advance
- Post breakdown: Instagram Reels (8/month), YouTube Shorts (4/month), Instagram
  Carousels (12/month), Instagram Static (16/month), Facebook posts (20/month)
- 10 nano-influencer collaborations per month: briefing, coordination, content review
- Community management on weekdays (comments responded within 4 hours)

Exclusions: Influencer fees are excluded from this proposal. We recommend a budget of
INR 5,000-15,000 per nano-influencer post based on current market rates.

Timeline: Content creation begins Week 14 (6 weeks before go-live); community
management from go-live.

LINE ITEM 5: Social Paid Media Planning
-----------------------------------------
Deliverables:
- Paid media strategy document
- Platform recommendation with rationale (Meta, YouTube, OTT — Hotstar and SonyLIV
  recommended)
- Audience targeting framework with 5 defined segments
- Budget allocation model (developed based on INR 1 Crore monthly media spend
  assumption; actual budget to be confirmed by client)
- KPI framework: Reach, frequency, video completion rate, website traffic, brand
  search lift

Note: The budget allocation model is built on an assumption of INR 1 Cr/month gross
media spend. If the actual budget differs significantly, the model will need revision.

Timeline: Weeks 10-12 from kickoff

LINE ITEM 6: Social Paid Media Buying and Optimization
--------------------------------------------------------
Deliverables:
- Campaign setup across Meta, Google/YouTube, and Hotstar
- Daily monitoring and weekly pacing reports
- Bi-weekly optimization review calls with NourishPlus team
- Monthly performance deep-dive with recommendations
- End-of-campaign summary report

Agency fee: Media buying is managed at a 10% commission on gross media spend, billed
monthly in arrears. This commission is NOT included in the INR 4,20,00,000 proposal
total. The line item cost of INR 30,00,000 covers the campaign management, reporting
infrastructure, and optimization team time.

Timeline: September 2025 – December 2025 (4 months ongoing)

LINE ITEM 7: Kids Advertising and Claims Compliance Review
-----------------------------------------------------------
Deliverables:
- ASCI Chapter III compliance review for all TV and digital creatives
- FSSAI nutrition and health claims validation checklist
- YouTube Kids and Meta under-13 policy review per platform
- Compliance sign-off documentation for each creative asset
- Pre-ASCI submission support if required for TVC clearance
- 1 compliance training session for NourishPlus marketing team

Team: Our dedicated compliance reviewer, Kavita Shah (ex-ASCI panelist, 15 years in
advertising compliance), leads this workstream. Kavita has cleared campaigns for
Nestle Milo, Britannia Bournvita, and Cadbury Bournvita in the past 24 months.

Timeline: Integrated throughout production and content creation (Weeks 6-20)

LINE ITEM 8: Launch Program Management
----------------------------------------
Deliverables:
- Project charter and RACI matrix
- Integrated master timeline with all dependencies
- Weekly status reports (standard format)
- Risk register with escalation matrix
- Fortnightly steering committee facilitation
- End-of-campaign review report

PM: Shreya Bose, PMP-certified, 9 years of campaign project management experience,
assigned full-time to NourishKids.

Timeline: Weeks 1-52 (full campaign duration)
