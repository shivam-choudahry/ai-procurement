SCOPE COVERAGE
================================================================================

LINE ITEM 1: Strategy & Creative Development — INCLUDED
---------------------------------------------------------
We'll develop a digital-first brand strategy:
- Social brand persona for NourishKids
- 3-4 content pillars (we'll refine at briefing)
- Messaging framework optimised for Meta and YouTube audiences
- Launch campaign concept for social-first activation

Note: Our strategy is optimised for digital channels. We are not an ATL/traditional
advertising agency. If broadcast TV is a significant component of the strategy, we'd
recommend supplementing with a traditional agency for that workstream.

LINE ITEM 2: TVC Development — INCLUDED (Online Video Format)
---------------------------------------------------------------
We develop high-quality digital video:
- 2 x hero online videos (90 seconds) for YouTube and Instagram
- 4 x cutdown versions (15 seconds each) for paid media
- Platform-adapted formats for Meta (9:16, 1:1) and YouTube (16:9)
- Storyboards and pre-production documentation

Clarification on scope: This covers online video development optimised for digital
platforms. We do not develop scripts or storyboards for traditional broadcast
television. If traditional broadcast TVC scripts are required in the RFQ format,
this is outside our scope.

LINE ITEM 3: TVC Production — PARTIAL COVERAGE
------------------------------------------------
We handle digital content production through our network of production partners:
- Product-focused digital shoots: INR 15-25 lakhs (depending on concept complexity)
- Lifestyle content and family shoots: INR 8-12 lakhs

Traditional broadcast TVC production for TV channels: This is not a core service for
Spark Digital. We have production partners who handle broadcast-quality shoots.
Estimated cost: INR 80-120 lakhs — however, this cannot be confirmed at this stage.
Final cost depends on creative brief, director selection, location, and number of
shoot days. We will be in a position to provide a firm quote after creative brief
finalisation and production house shortlisting, which typically happens in Week 4-5
of the project.

We recommend treating broadcast TVC production as a separate mini-RFQ once the
creative direction is confirmed.

LINE ITEM 4: Social Organic Content — INCLUDED
------------------------------------------------
- 50 posts/month across Instagram and Facebook
- Content calendar shared monthly
- Our recommendation: focus on Instagram and Facebook; we would suggest deprioritising
  YouTube organic for the first 6 months given the effort-to-impact ratio for a new
  brand
- 15 nano-influencer collaborations per month (briefing, content review, approval)

On volume: The RFQ mentions 60 posts/month. Based on our experience, 50 high-quality
posts outperform 60 mediocre posts. We'd welcome a discussion on this in the
presentation.

Influencer fees: Influencer compensation is billed separately based on platform rates,
which are typically USD 200-500 per nano-influencer post per platform. These will be
billed monthly in arrears based on actual rates.

LINE ITEM 5: Social Paid Media Planning — INCLUDED
----------------------------------------------------
- Full paid media strategy and channel plan
- Platform selection: Meta (Instagram + Facebook), Google/YouTube, and 1 OTT platform
- Audience targeting and segmentation framework
- Budget allocation and creative rotation plan
- KPI framework and attribution model

LINE ITEM 6: Social Paid Media Buying & Optimization — INCLUDED
-----------------------------------------------------------------
- Campaign setup and activation on Meta and Google ecosystems
- Daily monitoring with anomaly detection
- Weekly performance reports (dashboard format)
- Monthly optimisation review call

Fee structure: 12% on net media spend, billed monthly. This is in line with industry
practice for performance-focused agencies.

Note: We do not offer a flat monthly fee for media buying. Our model is
commission-based to align incentives with performance.

LINE ITEM 7: Kids Advertising & Claims Compliance — LIMITED SCOPE
------------------------------------------------------------------
We have extensive experience working with consumer brands that market to families and
children. Our creative team is well-versed in ensuring content is age-appropriate
and brand-safe.

For formal ASCI Kids Advertising Code compliance review and FSSAI health claims
validation, we recommend engaging a specialist compliance consultant. This is a
specialised regulatory area where a dedicated expert adds more value than a general
agency.

We can facilitate the engagement of a compliance consultant and coordinate the review
process. Facilitation fee: INR 5,00,000. Specialist consultant fees would be
additional and billed at cost.

We want to be transparent: formal regulatory compliance review is not a core
competency of Spark Digital. We want to set the right expectations rather than
overpromise on this.

LINE ITEM 8: Launch Program Management — INCLUDED (Streamlined)
-----------------------------------------------------------------
- Dedicated account manager (Arjun Reddy, Founder, actively involved in first 3 months)
- Fortnightly status calls with NourishPlus team
- Shared project tracker on Notion

Our PM approach is lean and digital-first. We use tools rather than heavy
documentation. This may differ from traditional agency PM models, but our clients
find it more responsive.
