SCOPE AND DELIVERABLES
================================================================================

LINE ITEM 1: Brand Strategy & Creative Development — INR 40,00,000
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Full brand strategy, creative platform development, 3 campaign concept routes, and
final creative direction. Includes synthesis of provided secondary research; primary
consumer research (if needed) would be additional.

LINE ITEM 2: TVC Development — INR 32,00,000
---------------------------------------------
Script, storyboard, and animatic for:
- 2 x 30-second TVC scripts (Hindi)
- 2 x 10-second cutdown scripts (Hindi)

English language TVC versions are available at an additional cost of INR 8,00,000.
Please advise if English language TVCs are required.

LINE ITEM 3: TVC Production — INR 1,60,00,000
-----------------------------------------------
Full production:
- 2 x 30-second TVCs (Hindi) + 2 x 10-second cutdowns
- Casting, location, direction, post-production
- Broadcast delivery formats

Note: Travel and accommodation for crew required outside Mumbai are charged at actuals
and are not included in the above cost.

LINE ITEM 4: Social Organic Content — INR 60,00,000
-----------------------------------------------------
- 45 posts/month for 6 months across Instagram (20/month), Facebook (15/month),
  YouTube (10/month)
- Content calendar and client approval workflow
- 8 nano/micro influencer collaborations per month

On volume: We are recommending 45 posts/month rather than 60. Based on platform
algorithm changes in 2024, quality and consistency outperform volume. We will discuss
this recommendation in the presentation.

Influencer fees: Included for nano-influencers only (posts valued up to INR 5,000
per post). Micro-influencer fees (INR 5,000-50,000 per post) are additional and
billed at actuals.

LINE ITEM 5: Social Paid Media Planning — INR 15,00,000
---------------------------------------------------------
Comprehensive paid media strategy, platform selection, audience framework, budget
model, and KPI plan.

LINE ITEM 6: Social Paid Media Buying & Optimization
------------------------------------------------------
We offer end-to-end paid media management. Given that this is included in our
all-inclusive package, the paid media buying and optimization service is part of our
integrated offering to NourishPlus.

On reflection, for a 6-month campaign at the expected scale, dedicated paid media
resources require separate allocation. Please note the paid media management fee below:

Paid Media Management Fee: INR 35,00,000
(6 months; campaign setup, daily management, weekly reporting, monthly review)

LINE ITEM 7: Kids Advertising & Claims Compliance — INR 12,00,000
-------------------------------------------------------------------
Brandsmith is ASCI compliant. Our creatives are always designed with regulatory
guidelines in mind. We have an internal review process for all advertising claims and
ensure all content is appropriate for the target audience.

LINE ITEM 8: Launch Program Management — INR 10,00,000
--------------------------------------------------------
- Dedicated project coordinator
- Weekly status reports
- Risk management and escalation
