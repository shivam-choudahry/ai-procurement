SCOPE COVERAGE
================================================================================

LINE ITEM 1: Strategy & Creative Development
---------------------------------------------
MediaPro can support with a digital brand and media strategy. We will develop:
- Digital channel strategy and platform prioritisation
- Audience framework and targeting architecture
- Content type and format recommendations for paid and organic

Note: We are a media-first agency. Brand strategy and full creative development
(positioning, messaging architecture, ATL creative) is outside our primary scope.
We recommend engaging a dedicated creative agency for the brand and ATL workstream.
Our contribution here is limited to digital strategy and media brief inputs.

LINE ITEM 2: TVC Development
------------------------------
This is not a service offered by MediaPro Solutions.

We are a digital and performance media agency. TVC script development, storyboarding,
and broadcast creative is outside our scope. We recommend engaging a creative agency
for this deliverable.

LINE ITEM 3: TVC Production
------------------------------
This is not a service offered by MediaPro Solutions.

TVC production and broadcast delivery is outside our scope. We do not manage film
production, casting, shoot direction, or broadcast delivery.

LINE ITEM 4: Social Organic Content — INCLUDED (Limited)
----------------------------------------------------------
MediaPro manages social content as a supporting service for paid media accounts.

We can deliver:
- Content calendar development and planning
- Up to 30 posts/month across Instagram and Facebook (static and basic graphic formats)
- Basic caption and copy writing
- Coordination with client creative team for video content

We do not have an in-house social content production capability for reels, YouTube
Shorts, or video-first formats. Video content would need to be provided by the client
or a separate content production partner.

Influencer management is outside our scope.

LINE ITEM 5: Social Paid Media Planning — CORE STRENGTH
---------------------------------------------------------
Full-service paid media planning:
- Complete channel strategy: Meta (Instagram, Facebook), Google (Search, Display,
  YouTube), OTT (Hotstar, SonyLIV, Zee5), Programmatic (DV360)
- Audience segmentation: 8 distinct audience cohorts mapped for NourishKids
  (metro parents, Tier-2 parents, health-conscious parents, child-influencer segment)
- Media mix modelling: reach/frequency targets by channel based on budget bands
- Budget allocation model with 3 scenarios (conservative INR 50L/month, base INR
  1Cr/month, aggressive INR 1.5Cr/month)
- KPI framework: reach, frequency, GRP equivalent, video views, CTR, website traffic,
  brand lift (if media budget enables brand lift survey)
- Competitive media audit for FMCG nutrition category

This is our strongest deliverable. Full strategy document typically 60-80 pages.

Timeline: 3-4 weeks from brief confirmation

LINE ITEM 6: Social Paid Media Buying & Optimization — CORE STRENGTH
----------------------------------------------------------------------
End-to-end paid media management across all platforms:
- Campaign architecture, audience building, creative trafficking
- Daily pacing, budget management, and anomaly detection
- Weekly performance dashboard (automated, live Looker Studio)
- Bi-weekly optimisation calls with NourishPlus marketing team
- Monthly deep-dive: spend, performance, attribution, recommendations
- A/B testing framework: 2 creative variants tested per ad set per month
- End-of-campaign report with attribution analysis

Fee structure: INR 12,00,000 per month flat fee for media management (6-month
engagement). This covers all platforms listed above. No percentage commission.

For context: at INR 1Cr/month media spend, our flat fee equates to a 12% effective
rate. At higher spends, the flat fee is better value for the client.

Setup fee (one-time): INR 3,50,000 for campaign architecture, pixel setup, audience
seeding, and platform account configuration.

LINE ITEM 7: Kids Advertising & Claims Compliance
---------------------------------------------------
MediaPro Solutions does not offer regulatory compliance services.

ASCI Kids Advertising Code review, FSSAI health claims validation, and platform
policy compliance (YouTube Kids, Meta under-13) are specialised regulatory areas.
We are a media agency — this is not our domain.

We recommend engaging a specialist regulatory consultant for this workstream. We are
happy to provide referrals if helpful.

LINE ITEM 8: Launch Program Management
----------------------------------------
We provide account management, not formal project management.

Our account management model:
- Dedicated account manager (Priya Varma) as single point of contact
- Fortnightly review calls
- Shared reporting dashboard accessible to client team at all times

Formal project management, RACI matrices, steering committees, and cross-agency
coordination are outside our standard offering.
